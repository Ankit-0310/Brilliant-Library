About Us

At Brilliant Library “Study Hub” in Kanpur, we strive to create an exceptional study atmosphere by providing all essential facilities. Our library offers a peaceful, sanitary, and stress-free setting that is equipped with modern amenities such as air conditioning, free WiFi, RO drinking water, individual cabins with power outlets, daily newspapers, power backup, CCTV surveillance, and more. Our aim is to provide you with an ideal environment for focused studying, where you can access all the necessary resources without any hassle.
